NOKERA
platform

NOKERA's tech-based platform combines planning, construction, installation and services in a consistent system. We offer a seamless, sustainable, and scalable process from the beginning to the end of a project.

Design

Our integrated architecture and engineer offices and a think tank-controlled product development provide urban planning concepts of the future. The implemented BIM software enables the early detection of conflicts and increases the efficiency of the planning.

Distribution

The organization of our supply chain is determined by the focus on efficiency. This ensures that each delivery always arrives at exactly the right time. A continuous flow of data serves as the basis of our platform and allows processes to run seamlessly into one another.

Sustainment

Through the smart use of data, we develop value-added services in building management, energy supply or in the media sector. This is how we increase the value of the property throughout its entire life cycle.

We provide manufacturing processes for the modern construction industry. NOKERA works with offsite high-tech production facilities, which function through digitalization, automation, and robotization. We particularly opt for sustainable resources such as wood in the manufacture of components.

Production

Our NOKERA teams or selected partner companies ensure that the installation runs smoothly and correctly on-site. Thanks to our Plug & Build principle, we guarantee a higher construction output, lower staff requirements, and a considerable reduction in waste.

Construction

We are combining unique offsite manufacturing capabilities with technology know-how in one integrated platform. Up to 80% prefabrication and highly standardized components lay the ground for hyper-scale for every project. Nokera provides end-to-end building services throughout the entire building life cycle.

Our offer does not end with a finished building. We create long-term value by adding services throughout the entire property life cycle.

Building
management

NOKERA offers suitable services for every property. We work with janitors and provide green space or winter road maintenance. We are also happy to organize the maintenance of the building technologies like heating or locking systems.

Energy
supply

NOKERA takes care of the basic electricity and gas supply for every property. But more than that, NOKERA generates sustainable energy from photovoltaic systems, from which households and the environment benefit even more.

Media

NOKERA either supplies households directly with Internet and TV connections or works with reliable partner companies who are happy to provide the right media.

NOKERA
App

Our app offers additional services such as building management, repair, laundry and delivery services, security management, and car-sharing. Besides, the app makes it easy for tenants and landlords to communicate, which simplifies for example damage reports.
